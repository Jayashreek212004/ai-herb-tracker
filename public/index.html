🌿 About Us

At Anvetha,we are dedicated to ensuring complete traceability of herbal products, right from farm to consumer. As part of the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) initiative, our goal is to provide transparency, quality assurance, and trust in every product you purchase. By combining traditional knowledge of Ayurveda with modern technology, we make sure every herbal product is authentic, safe, and effective.Our platform allows customers to track the journey of each batch using a unique QR code, providing detailed insights into the cultivation, harvesting, processing, and delivery of the herbs.

Our Tracking Journey

  • 🌱 Cultivation: Herbs grown on certified organic farms.
  • 📝 Harvesting: Each batch inspected for quality.
  • 🏭 Processing: Herbs processed safely.
  • 🔍 Traceability: Unique QR for each batch.
  • 📦 Delivery: Scan QR to see journey.
